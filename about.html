About

Hi, I'm Joseph Suh and I work as a game developer.

I graduated with a computer science degree from the University of Waterloo in 2019. I did several software engineering internships during that time and worked on a lot of different things (apps, games, websites, firmware). Most recently, I have been working at Behaviour Interactive as a programmer for the game Dead by Daylight. I also made a small action game for Android devices which you can play here.

Outside of programming, I also like to do figure drawing occasionally. I'm often occupied by full-time work, but hopefully I'll have some time to post art eventually.
